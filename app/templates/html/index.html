About Us

The World Assemblies of God Fellowship is a global Pentecostal denomination made up of independent yet united member churches sharing the same beliefs and history.

The General Council of the Assemblies of God, Nigeria, as part of this fellowship, is dedicated to spreading the gospel in Nigeria and beyond through the power of the Holy Spirit. It seeks to foster worship, strengthen fellowship among believers, and support educational efforts for the church’s growth and effectiveness.
